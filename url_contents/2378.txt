			<h1 class="page-title">Certificate in German Language</h1>



<div id="textcontainer" class="tab_content" role="tabpanel">

<div class="sidebar">
	<div id="sidebar-contact">








	</div>
</div>

<p>The Certificate in German Language provides students interested in developing higher‐level <span>proficiency in the language with the opportunity to certify their acquisition of those skills. Students </span><span>with basic listening, oral, and writing skills, as taught in the beginning‐level courses, can satisfy the </span><span>requirements of the certificate by taking two classes at the intermediate level to further develop </span><span>these fundamental language skills, followed by three classes at a more advanced level, which allow </span><span>students to polish language skills and raise proficiency to a level suitable for purposeful and effective </span><span>communication in everyday German‐speaking environments as well as German professional </span><span>settings.</span></p>
<p>Students from other disciplines who want to pursue the Certificate in German Language must <span>complete a minimum of 17 semester hours, as outlined below:</span></p>
