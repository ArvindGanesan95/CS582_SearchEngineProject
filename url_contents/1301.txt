			<h1 class="page-title">Program Changes and Updates</h1>



<div id="textcontainer" class="tab_content" role="tabpanel">

<div class="sidebar">
	<div id="sidebar-contact">








	</div>
</div>

<p>The online catalog is updated as program and requirement changes are approved. If the same program is revised more than once, the new requirements replace previous requirements. All of the links below refer users to the current requirements.</p>
<h2><strong>Spring 2021</strong></h2>
<h3>College of Applied Health Sciences</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Establish a <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/applied-health-sciences/kn/minor-food-science/">Minor in Food Science</a></li>
</ul>
<h3>​College of Business Administration</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Rename and Revise the Concentration in Marketing Research; Revise the Concentration in Sales and Leadership; Rename and Revise the Concentration in Innovation and Product Management; Establish the Concentration in Retail and Services Marketing in the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/business-administration/marketing/bs/">BS in Marketing</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/business-administration/mgmt/bs-mgmt/">BS in Management</a>, Establish the Concentration in Management Consulting, and Eliminate the Concentration in Strategic Management, Rename the Concentration in Leadership and Managerial Skills</li>
	<li>Rename and Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/business-administration/minors/minor-mgr-skill/">Minor in Managerial Skills</a></li>
</ul>
<h3><strong>College of Engineering</strong></h3>
<ul>
	<li>
	<p>Revise the Technical Electives Requirement in the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/engineering/cme/bs-cive/">Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering</a></p>
	</li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/engineering/ece/bs-eng-phys/">BS in Engineering Physics</a></li>
</ul>
<h3>College of Liberal Arts and Sciences</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/liberal-arts-sciences/mscs/bs-teach-math/">BS in the Teaching of Mathematics</a></li>
</ul>
<h3>College of Urban Planning and Public Affairs</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Establish a <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/urban-planning-public-affairs/pa/minor-civic-analytics/">Minor in Civic Analytics</a></li>
	<li>Establish a <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/urban-planning-public-affairs/pa/minor-nonprofit-management/">Minor in Nonprofit Management</a></li>
	<li>Establish the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/urban-planning-public-affairs/pa/joint-degrees/">Joint BA in Public Policy/Master of Public Policy</a>, the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/urban-planning-public-affairs/pa/joint-degrees/">Joint BA in Public Policy/Master of Urban Planning and Policy</a>, and the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/urban-planning-public-affairs/pa/joint-degrees/">Joint BA in Public Policy/MS in Civic Analytics</a></li>
	<li>Establish the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/urban-planning-public-affairs/upp/joint-degrees/">Joint BA in Urban Studies/Master of Public Administration</a>, the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/urban-planning-public-affairs/upp/joint-degrees/">Joint BA in Urban Studies/Master of Public Policy</a>, and the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/urban-planning-public-affairs/upp/joint-degrees/">Joint BA in Urban Studies/MS in Civic Analytics</a></li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>Spring 2020</strong></h2>
<ul>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/degree-programs/univ-degree-requirements-graduation/#enrollment-residence-requirement">Enrollment Residence Requirement</a> for Undergraduate Degrees</li>
</ul>
<h3>College of Applied Health Sciences</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/applied-health-sciences/kn/mino-nutr/">Minor in Nutrition</a></li>
</ul>
<h3>College of Architecture, Design, and the Arts</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/architecture-design-arts/des/bdes-gd/">BDes in Graphic Design</a> and the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/architecture-design-arts/des/bdes-id/">BDes in Industrial Design</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/liberal-arts-sciences/anth/ba/">BA in Liberal Arts and Sciences, Major in Anthropology</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/colleges-depts/liberal-arts-sciences/ihs/">BS in Liberal Arts and Sciences, Major in Integrated Health Studies</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/liberal-arts-sciences/soc/ba/">BA in Liberal Arts and Sciences, Major in Sociology</a></li>
</ul>
<h3>College of Business Administration</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/business-administration/actg/bs/">BS in Accounting</a></li>
</ul>
<h3>College of Liberal Arts and Sciences</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Establish the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/liberal-arts-sciences/slav/certificate-polish/">Undergraduate Campus Certificate in Polish Language</a></li>
	<li>Establish the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/liberal-arts-sciences/slav/certificate-russian/">Undergraduate Campus Certificate in Russian Language</a></li>
</ul>
<h3>College of Urban Planning and Policy</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/urban-planning-public-affairs/upp/minor-gis/">Minor in Geographic Information Systems</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/urban-planning-public-affairs/upp/minor-sc/">Minor in Sustainable Cities</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/urban-planning-public-affairs/upp/minor-upp/">Minor in Urban Studies</a></li>
</ul>
<h2>Summer 2020</h2>
<h3>College of Liberal Arts and Sciences</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/liberal-arts-sciences/psych/ba-psych-gp-conc/">BA in Liberal Arts and Sciences, Major in Psychology, General Psychology Concentration</a> and <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/liberal-arts-sciences/psych/ba-psych-ap-conc/">BA in Liberal Arts and Sciences, Major in Psychology, Applied Psychology Concentration</a></li>
	<li><span>Establish the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/liberal-arts-sciences/anth/certificate-medical-humanities/">Undergraduate Campus Certificate in Medical <span>Humanities</span></a></span></li>
</ul>
<h2><span><a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/liberal-arts-sciences/anth/certificate-medical-humanities/"><span>​</span></a></span>Fall 2020</h2>
<h3>College of Applied Health Sciences</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Establish a <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/applied-health-sciences/#d2">Required First-Year Seminar in the College of Applied Health Sciences</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/applied-health-sciences/dhd/bs/">BS in Disability and Human Development</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/applied-health-sciences/kn/bs-nutr-cp-conc/">BS in Nutrition, Coordinated Program Concentration</a> and the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/applied-health-sciences/kn/bs-nutr-nsc-conc/">BS in Nutrition, Nutrition Science Concentration</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/applied-health-sciences/kn/mino-nutr/">Minor in Nutrition</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/applied-health-sciences/bhis/lsv-minor/">Minor in Life Science Visualization</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/applied-health-sciences/kn/minor-kn/">Minor in Kinesiology</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/applied-health-sciences/kn/bs-kn/">Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology</a> and Eliminate the Concentration in Exercise Science and Health Promotion and Concentration in Movement Science</li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/architecture-design-arts/arch/ba-arch/">BA in Architectural Studies</a></li>
</ul>
<h3>College of Architecture, Design, and the Arts</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/architecture-design-arts/art-ah/minor-art/">Minor in Art</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/architecture-design-arts/thtr-mus/bfa-act/">BFA in Acting</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/architecture-design-arts/thtr-mus/ba-act/">BA in Theatre and Performance</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/architecture-design-arts/thtr-mus/ba-thtr-des/">BA in Theatre Design, Production, and Technology</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/architecture-design-arts/art-ah/ba-ida/">BA in Interdisciplinary Education in the Arts</a></li>
</ul>
<h3>College of Business Administration</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/business-administration/mgmt/bs-mktg/">BS in Marketing</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/business-administration/fin/bs/">BS in Finance</a></li>
	<li>Establish the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/business-administration/marketing/">Department of Marketing</a></li>
</ul>
<h3>College of Engineering</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/engineering/cme/bs-cive/">BS in Civil Engineering</a></li>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/engineering/che/bs-che/">BS in Chemical Engineering</a></li>
</ul>
<h3>College of Liberal Arts and Sciences</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Revise the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/liberal-arts-sciences/lals/ba/">BS in Liberal Arts and Sciences, Major in Latin American and Latino Studies</a></li>
