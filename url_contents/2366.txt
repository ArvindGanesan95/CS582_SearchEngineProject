<h2>Degree Requirements—BA in Theatre and Performance</h2>
<p>To earn a Bachelor of Arts in Theatre and Performance from UIC, students need to complete university, college, and school degree requirements. The School of Theatre &amp; Music degree requirements are outlined below. Students should consult the <a href="/ucat/colleges-depts/architecture-design-arts/"><em>College of Architecture, Design, and the Arts</em></a> section for additional degree requirements and college academic policies. Admission to the BA in Theatre and Performance is by audition, interview, or presentation of student work.</p>
