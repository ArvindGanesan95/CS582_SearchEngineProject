              <h3>Administrative Information Technology Services (AITS)</h3>
<p>AITS is the IT unit responsible for enterprise-wide information technology solutions and services in support of the administrative processes of the University of Illinois System (UIC, UIUC and UIS). AITS has 8 departments that support U of I of Systems such as Banner, human resources, student registration, financial management, enterprise-wide services, and more.</p>
