                            <h1>Short URLs</h1>
                            <p style="font-size:100%;line-height:140%;">The Short URL Webtool allows you to take a long URL and create a short one that points to it. 
                              Short URLs are great for print or emailing or displaying on a Web page. If the long URL changes, simply update it in the Webtools; no need to reprint marketing material or resend emails. 
                              It also has a reporting feature that lets you know when the link has been clicked. <br>
                              <br>
                              <strong>Login to the Webtools at:</strong><br>
