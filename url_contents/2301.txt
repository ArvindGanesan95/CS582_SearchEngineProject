			<h1 class="page-title">Minor in Art History</h1>



<div id="textcontainer" class="tab_content" role="tabpanel">

<div class="sidebar">
	<div id="sidebar-contact">








	</div>
</div>

<p>A minimum of 20 semester hours in art history courses, of which at least 10 hours must be taken at the University of Illinois at Chicago. Students may elect one additional 100-level course to count as an AH elective, excluding <span class="sc_courseinline"><a href="/search/?P=AH%20100" title="AH 100" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'AH 100');">AH 100</a></span>. </p>
