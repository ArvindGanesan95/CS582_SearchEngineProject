			<h1 class="page-title">Minor in Food Science</h1>



<div id="textcontainer" class="tab_content" role="tabpanel">

<div class="sidebar">
	<div id="sidebar-contact">








	</div>
</div>

<p>The Minor in Food Science is open to undergraduate students in any college who complete both prerequisite and required course work. Students will be allowed to complete the minor if they have a minimum GPA of 2.50/4.00 at the time of application. Students must submit an application. Students must also consult their home colleges about the acceptability and applicability of Food Science course credit toward their degree. Registration for most courses in the food science curriculum is restricted; therefore, students must register through a nutrition department academic advisor. A minimum cumulative GPA of 2.50/4.00 is required for the minor field.</p>
