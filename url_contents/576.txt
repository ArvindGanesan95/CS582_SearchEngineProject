                    <h2 id="study-english-on-the-uic-campus" class="__title invisible">Study English on the UIC Campus</h2>
        
            </div>


    <figure class="_image captioned-image image-right-half">
  <img src="https://tie.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/403/2021/02/OGE-Dec-Collage-1090x774.jpg" alt="a large group of happy tutorium students">
  </figure>
  
  <h2>Your Path to Academic Success</h2>
<p>For more than 40 years, our highly qualified and experienced <a href="https://tie.uic.edu/about-the-tutorium/our-team/">staff and instructors</a> have helped students of all levels, from all backgrounds, learn English to achieve their academic, professional and personal goals.</p>
<p>As part of the University of Illinois at Chicago, the Tutorium can help you progress from full-time study in our <a href="https://tie.uic.edu/intensive-english/">Intensive English Program</a> to an undergraduate or graduate degree at University of Illinois at Chicago.</p>
<h3>The Tutorium provides:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Expert, experienced instructors in a supportive environment</li>
<li>A <a href="https://tie.uic.edu/student-life/">diverse community</a> of international students on a vibrant campus</li>
<li><a href="https://tie.uic.edu/about-the-tutorium/pathway/">Paths to progress</a> to an undergraduate or graduate degree at UIC</li>
</ul>
<p><a href="https://michalsk.youcanbook.me/">Click to schedule an online appointment with our Admissions/Advising Team</a></p>
<h5>Summer 2021 courses are online.</h5>
<h5>Fall 2021 courses have on campus and hybrid options.</h5>
