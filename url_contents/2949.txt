                    <h2 id="about-campuscare" class="__title invisible">About CampusCare</h2>
        
            </div>


  
  <p><em>CampusCare</em> is an affordable self-funded student health benefit program that has been providing comprehensive health care benefits to eligible enrolled students and their covered dependents at the University of Illinois Chicago, Rockford and Peoria campuses since 2004.</p>
<div>
<p>The University of Illinois requires all students to be covered by major medical health insurance, therefore all students are automatically enrolled in CampusCare and assessed the CampusCare fee on their tuition when registering for courses that are charged with tuition &amp; mandatory fees. There are no enrollment forms to complete. CampusCare qualifies for minimal essential coverage under the Affordable Care Act as a self-funded health benefit program and is not a licensed insurance company.</p>
<div>Eligible students include all registered Undergraduate, Graduate, and Health Professional students taking at least one class that charges them with tuition and mandatory fees. Eligible fellows working under a T/32 or F/32 grant at UIC are also eligible for <em>CampusCare </em>(to verify if you are eligible please contact your program administrator). To verify if you are a member, students can view the <em>CampusCare</em> charge by going to their student account bill (E-Bill). If you are not charged with the <em>CampusCare</em> Fee you are not a member of <em>CampusCare. “</em>Online only” students and “Option B” students are NOT eligible for <em>CampusCare. <strong>**Please note: Online only students who are charged with tuition &amp; all other fees will be charged the CampusCare fee, due to the COVID-19 pandemic, therefore a waiver must be submitted with your current Health Insurance information to remove our fee.**</strong></em></div>
</div>
<p><em>CampusCare</em> does not provide an extension of coverage or Cobra policy for Fall or Spring semesters. Students who are taking a leave of absence or graduating cannot continue coverage to the next semester in Fall or Spring. Please refer to the <a href="https://campuscare.uic.edu/important-information/summer-coverage/">Summer Coverage</a> page for more information regarding your <em>CampusCare </em>coverage options.</p>
<p>Students with comparable health insurance coverage, for example through a legal guardian or an employer, can waive out of the program by submitting the online <a href="https://mycampuscare.uic.edu/login/">Waiver Form</a> before the Enrollment Period <a href="https://campuscare.uic.edu/important-information/dates-fees/">Deadline Date</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Please Note:<em> CampusCare</em> is your health benefits coverage program, we are NOT a clinic where you can obtain medical services. Please contact your <a href="https://campuscare.uic.edu/benefits/primary-care/"><em>CampusCare</em> Primary Care Provider&#8217;s office</a> for all appointments, medical issues, or medical questions.</strong></p>
<p>Important features of the <em>CampusCare</em> program:</p>
<ul>
<li>All covered services must be provided or authorized by a <a href="https://campuscare.uic.edu/benefits/primary-care/"><em>CampusCare</em> Health Primary Care Provider</a>, unless they meet the Medical Emergency guidelines as stated in the <a href="https://campuscare.uic.edu/policies/certificate-of-coverage/">Certificate of Coverage</a> or obtain a preauthorized referral by the <em>CampusCare</em> Medical Director or designee.</li>
<li>All services provided outside a designated <em>CampusCare</em> Health Center require an authorized referral prior to receiving services. Referrals will not cover services retroactively.</li>
<li>Unauthorized services provided elsewhere are the enrolled member’s full financial responsibility.</li>
<li><em>CampusCare</em> is considered a <a href="https://campuscare.uic.edu/important-information/campuscare-as-secondary-insurance/">secondary payer </a>to all health insurance plans, except in the case a member has Medicaid/Public Aid, then <em>CampusCare</em> will be the primary payer. In all cases <em>CampusCare</em> will only provide payment as stated in the Certificate of Coverage.</li>
</ul>

<p><em>CampusCare</em> only covers health services provided in the United States. In addition to the required international health insurance coverage, students studying abroad must have domestic coverage in order to waive out of <em>CampusCare. CampusCare</em> coverage can be continued during semesters abroad to provide domestic health coverage in the event it is needed.</p>
<p>Students participating in a recognized Study Abroad Program who have not previously waived <em>CampusCare</em> coverage will be automatically enrolled (along with any enrolled dependents) in <em>CampusCare</em> for their term abroad and assessed the Student Health Insurance Fee.</p>
<p>Students who do not wish to have concurrent domestic health benefits coverage under <em>CampusCare</em> during their term abroad must submit a <a href="https://campuscare.red.uic.edu/important-information/waiver-reinstatement-forms/">Waiver Form</a> before the Enrollment Period <a href="https://campuscare.uic.edu/important-information/dates-fees/">Deadline Date</a>.</p>
<p>Students who previously waived out of <em>CampusCare</em> but wish to have concurrent domestic coverage under <em>CampusCare</em> during their term abroad should submit a <a href="https://campuscare.red.uic.edu/important-information/waiver-reinstatement-forms/">Reinstatement Form</a> before the Enrollment Period <a href="https://campuscare.red.uic.edu/important-information/dates-fees/">Deadline Date</a>. Students may also enroll eligible dependents by submitting the <a href="https://campuscare.red.uic.edu/dependent-information/">Dependent Enrollment Form</a> before the Enrollment Period <a href="https://campuscare.red.uic.edu/important-information/dates-fees/">Deadline Date</a>.</p>
<p>Questions regarding international health insurance coverage may be directed to the UIC Study Abroad Office at 312-413-7662.</p>
<p>Covered <em>CampusCare</em> members have additional emergency medical assistance through the WorldNet Travel Assistance Program when studying abroad. To find out more about services provided under the WorldNet program call 800-290-7602 or visit <a href="http://uoi.mymemberinfo.com/insurance-benefits.aspx#3">http://uoi.mymemberinfo.com/insurance-benefits.aspx#3</a>.</p>
