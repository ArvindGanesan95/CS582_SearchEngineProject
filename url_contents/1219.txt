                      <h1 class="_title post-title">Alternate Format Request Form</h1>
              
        
        
              
    
              <p>In order to ensure receipt of accessible materials in a timely manner, <strong>students must complete the following form at least two weeks in advance</strong> of when the text is needed. In rare instances, document conversion may take longer than two weeks; in these cases the DRC will be in contact with the student and their instructor about timeline.</p>
<p>Once requested materials are ready for use, they will either be uploaded to your Kurzweil Universal Library or sent to you via the UIC cloud platform, Box. For a tutorial on setting up a Box account for the first time, follow this link: <a href="https://accc.uic.edu/answer/how-do-i-create-university-box-account">Link to Box Set-Up Instructions.</a></p>
<p>Feel free to reach out to the document conversion team with any questions or concerns at any time by emailing accesstext@uic.edu.</p>
