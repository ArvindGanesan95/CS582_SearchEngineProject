<div class="entry-meta"><span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">By </span><span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="https://libraryblog.jmls.uic.edu/author/pjohnso/" title="View all posts by Philip Johnson">Philip Johnson</a></span><span class="meta-sep meta-sep-entry-date"> | </span><span class="meta-prep meta-prep-entry-date">Published: </span><span class="entry-date"><abbr class="published" title="2020-08-24T11:51:11-0500">August 24, 2020</abbr></span></div><!-- .entry-meta -->

<p>While the UIC Law campus is closed to attorneys and alumni, Westlaw is allowing 14 days of access to non-law school patrons. See the registration instructions <a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="https://libraryblog.jmls.uic.edu/patron-access-instructions-august-2020/" target="_blank">here</a>, and register <a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="https://legal.thomsonreuters.com/en/c/remote-patron-access" target="_blank">here</a>. Note that the email address you use to register must not already be associated with a Thomson Reuters account.</p>
